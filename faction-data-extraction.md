## Lore & Theme

- **Theme:** "Dragon-bonded aerial warriors who wield dragonfire and soar on drake-back"
- **Flavor Text:** "The sky belongs to the Warpack. From burning nests in the Scorched Peaks, the Emberclaw ride drakes born of molten stone and feral flame. Every warrior is bonded to a drake ‚Äî a partnership sealed in fire that grants both rider and beast unnatural strength. - **Core Philosophy:** "Strength through bonding; power through flame; glory through the skies."
- **Motif:** "Flame sigils, dragon iconography, scorched earth"
- **Worldview:** "The sky is the only honest place ‚Äî on the ground, cowards hide behind walls. In the air, only strength and flame decide."

## Political Structure

- **Government:** Ember Council ‚Äî 5 Flamewarden elders who have each survived three bonding cycles
- **Hierarchy:** Flamewarden ‚Üí Scorchcaller ‚Üí Ashbringer ‚Üí Kindled ‚Üí Unbonded
- **Lore Leader:** The Crimson Tyrant ‚Äî a divine ancestor said to be the First Drake, now a semi-mythic figure whose flame allegedly still burns in the deepest nest

## Doctrine: "Flame Creed"

1. **The First Flame:** All fire is sacred; every blaze is a prayer
2. **The Bond Unbroken:** A rider who abandons their drake is worse than dead ‚Äî they are forgotten
3. **The Ascent:** The higher you fly, the closer to truth; the ground is for the unworthy

## Playstyle

Aerial dominance, alpha strikes, breath weapons, Drake Bonds. Vulnerable to massed ranged fire and anti-air.

## Faction Bonuses

- **Fly** ignores terrain
- **Breath Weapon** ignores cover
- **Drake Bond** (+1 ATK, share wounds between bonded pairs)
- **Flame Aura** (adjacent enemies take damage)

## Faction Mechanic: Heat Pool

- Heat generated by Fire keyword units, Breath Weapons, Drake Bond activations, fire kills
- Spend Heat for Superheated Strikes, extra Breath Weapons, Thermal Updraft, Firestorm, Fragment activations
- **Overheat** at 15+: all units take 1 damage, Commander can't play Fragment cards next turn, Fire attacks deal +1 damage
- Heat decays by 3 at start of each Command Phase

**Fragment Charges:** Units near War Machines/Support generate 1 charge/turn. Spend to activate fragments. Instability roll after activation.

## Combat Resolution

## Combat Modifiers
- Flanking: +1 ATK die
- Rear attack: +2 ATK dice
- Light Cover: +1 DEF vs ranged
- Heavy Cover: +2 DEF vs ranged
- Elevated: +1 ATK die (ranged)
- Charging (5"+ straight line): +1 ATK die (melee)
- Shaken: -1 ATK die
- Grid Active (Iron Dominion): +1 ATK die
- Grid Fortified: +1 ATK and +1 DEF
- Honor Stance (Veilbound): +1 DEF, -1 ATK, no flanking
- Revelation Stance (Veilbound): +1 ATK, -1 DEF
- Commander Aura (8"): +1 MOR

## Victory Conditions
- **Annihilation:** Destroy enemy Commander
- **Objective Control:** Control majority of 3 objectives after final turn
- **Attrition:** Destroy most enemy points over full game
- **King of the Hill:** Score VP each turn for controlling center objective (first to 5)
