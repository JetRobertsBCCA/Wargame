Gameplay Mechanics

1. Army Building

Points-Based System

Each unit and war machine has a point cost. Players build armies within a chosen point limit:

  • Skirmish: 50–100 points
  • Medium battle: 200–300 points
  • Epic multi-day battle: 500+ points

Composition Rules

  • Minimum and maximum number of units can be optionally set by scenario
  • Signature units of commanders are optional but reinforce the commander's unique strategy
  • Units are faction-cohesive: human/clockwork, steampunk, fragment-infused
  • Fragments affect army-wide interactions depending on evolution path

Army Builder Tool

Player selects:

  • Commander
  • Units (from standard units)
  • War Machines

Displays points used, points remaining, and faction cohesion. Shows fragment interactions based on commander evolution. Save / load army templates.

2. Commander RPG Progression

Leveling System

XP gained from:

  • Battle victories
  • Unit survival
  • Fragment activations
  • Objectives / mission completion

Level-Up Mechanics

  • Levels 2–10: Player selects skills branching into Knowledge, Chaos, or Tactical paths
  • Level 10 Evolution: Commander evolves into Knowledge / Chaos / Hybrid, unlocking:
    • New cards / abilities
    • Modified fragment interactions
    • Enhanced unit synergies

Commander Cards

  • Command Cards: Coordinate multiple units, boost morale, trigger tactical maneuvers
  • Tech Cards: Overclock war machines, repair, enhance unit stats
  • Fragment Cards: Activate fragment effects, warp battlefield, mutate units
  • Tactical Cards: Extra unit movement, precision strikes, temporary buffs

3. Tactical Layer (Zoom-In Skirmishes)

When elite units or special events trigger, players zoom into individual tactical encounters.

Units' stats determine outcomes: attack, defense, movement, special abilities.

Commander Card Deployment

  • Knowledge evolution: Controlled, precise effects
  • Chaos evolution: Powerful, unpredictable effects
  • Hybrid evolution: Moderate effects, occasional instability

Tactical Outcomes Feed Back Into Army Layer

  • Morale changes
  • Unit damage
  • Fragment instability

4. Fragment Mechanics

  • Activation: Triggered by units or commander cards
  • Scaling: Effects depend on commander evolution (Knowledge / Chaos / Hybrid)
  • Interactions: Certain fragments synergize with specific units or war machines

Risk / Reward

  • Knowledge: Controlled buffs/debuffs
  • Chaos: Strong but unstable effects
  • Hybrid: Moderate effects with occasional surprises

Field Effects

Terrain warp, AoE damage, temporary buffs, or fragment-induced mutations.

5. Multi-Day Campaign Tracking

  • XP, level-ups, and fragment choices persist across battles
  • Unit persistence: damage, buffs, fragment mutations carry over
  • Evolution paths permanently alter commander abilities for campaign

Optional Campaign Rules

  • Save / load campaigns
  • Track outcomes for multiple players
  • Scaling for massive multi-day engagements

6. Wiki Features to Support Gameplay

  • Interactive army builder
  • Commander skill tree interface
  • Fragment effect tracker
  • Searchable / filterable database: Factions, units, commanders, war machines, fragment types
  • Cross-links between commanders, signature units, and fragments
  • Placeholders for unit art for player customization
  • Version control: easy to add new factions, units, commanders, or fragments
